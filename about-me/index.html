About Me

I am currently a graduate research assistant at the University of Texas at Dallas, working with Dr.Gopal Gupta and Dr.Shiyi Wei. My research interests include Explainable AI (XAI), Machine Learning (ML), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Software Engineering (SWE). Additionally, I have explored debugging ML models in NLP, Computer Vision (CV), and SWE by analyzing and explaining their behavior. My work also involves developing ML/NLP models for various applications, such as an evolving programming assistant tool, a video compression scheme using CV models, and an action recognition system.
